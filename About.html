ABOUT US

How It Was Founded

This company was founded in 2020 by Janet Antony with the aim of promoting and selling fashion and beauty products to a wide variety of countries. It also aimed to provide opportunities to young girls who want to join the fashion industry.

Founder of Fashion House

Our Mission

Our fashion company aims to create stylish and high-quality clothing that reflects the unique personality and individuality of each customer. We strive to provide exceptional customer service, and affordable pricing, so that everyone can enjoy our fashion lines

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading fashion brand that is known for its ethically-sourced, sustainable and innovative products. We aspire to inspire confidence in our customers by creating fashion that empowers and celebrates their individuality. Our goal is to make fashion accessible to everyone, regardless of shape, size or budget with our inclusive and diverse collections. We envision a world where fashion is not just a form of self-expression, but also a way to support causes that matter to our customers.
